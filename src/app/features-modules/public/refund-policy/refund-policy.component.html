Soowgood Refund and Return Policy

At Soowgood, we prioritize your satisfaction, and our flexible return and refund policy ensure a smooth experience across our services.

Doctor Consultation:

  • To reschedule or cancel a pre-scheduled doctor consultation, please notify us at least 3 hours before the appointment.
  • Cancellation: Receive 80% of consultation fees as a refund, processed within 7 working days.
  • Rescheduling: A new suitable time will be arranged in consultation with you.

Medicine Home Delivery:

  • Carefully check your delivery upon arrival; Soowgood is not liable once the delivery agent leaves.
  • Return Policy: Medicines cannot be returned after 7 days from the delivery date.
  • Special Cases: Refrigerated medicines like insulin are non-returnable.

Home Sample Collection:

  • For rescheduling or canceling home sample collection, notify us at least 3 hours before the appointment.
  • Cancellation: Entitled to 80% of the booking fees as a refund, disbursed within 7 working days.
  • Rescheduling: We'll coordinate a new suitable time that suits you.

Healthcare Package:

  • Refunds for healthcare packages are evaluated on a case-by-case basis, considering the nature of the package and services utilized.
  • Refund requests must be submitted within 7 days of the purchase date.

Physiotherapy and Nursing Services:

  • To reschedule or cancel Physiotherapy and Nursing Services, notify us at least 3 hours before the appointment.
  • Cancellation: Receive 80% of the booking fees as a refund, processed within 7 working days.
  • Rescheduling: We'll collaborate to find a new suitable appointment time.
