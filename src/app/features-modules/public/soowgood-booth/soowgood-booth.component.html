Village Digital Booth

To bring quality healthcare services to the rural communities of the country, Soowgood collaborates with Zaytoon Business Solution, working through the Village Digital Booth. Through this initiative, patients can easily receive expert medical advice from experienced doctors located in their proximity.

Services:

  • Affordable and quality healthcare;
  • Consultation with specialized and experienced doctors;
  • Instant communication with doctors through video calls;
  • Specialized doctors available for women and children;
  • Digital prescriptions;
  • Convenient appointment scheduling with doctors at preferred times;
  • Personal and medical information and consultation documents will be secure and confidential;
  • Additionally, services include ambulance service, blood donation, home sample collection, medicine home delivery, healthcare packages, physiotherapy, and nursing services;
  • 24/7 online customer support.
