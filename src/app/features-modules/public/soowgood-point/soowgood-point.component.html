Soowgood Point

Soowgood Point, a new initiative of Soowgood, aims to provide modern and comprehensive healthcare services to remote areas of the country through online.

Services:

  • Affordable and quality healthcare;
  • Consultation with specialized and experienced doctors;
  • Instant communication with doctors through video calls;
  • Specialized doctors available for women and children;
  • Digital prescriptions;
  • Convenient appointment scheduling with doctors at preferred times;
  • Personal and medical information and consultation documents will be secure and confidential;
  • 24/7 online customer support.

Benefits for Entrepreneurs/Partners:

  • Additional income opportunities;
  • No need for any collateral to become a partner;
  • Anyone, including individuals at local businesses like pharmacies, bKash, photocopy shops, or grocery stores, can be part of this initiative. However, they must have internet connectivity, a computer or laptop or mobile phone, and a designated space for operation.
